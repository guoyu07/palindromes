SPEC 1:  A single word spelled the same both backwards and forwards is a palindrome
Input:  bob
Output:  TRUE

SPEC 2:  There must be at least 2 characters to be identified as a palindrome
Input:  a
Output:  FALSE

SPEC 3:  A full phrase may be a palindrome
Input:  Ed is on no side
Output:  TRUE

SPEC 4:  A palindrome may include all alphanumeric characters
Input:  101
Output:  TRUE
